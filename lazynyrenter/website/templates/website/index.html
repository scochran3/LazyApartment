Who or what are we?

We provide data on apartments in New York City that let you gauge price by borough, neighborhood or even zip code. The best part? It's all free. Every day (really every hour but let's not get too wonky) we gather data from Craigslist apartment listings in New York and turn that into useful data for you. Ever wonder what the average price of an apartment in your hood is? Or how much will a 1 vs. a 2 bedroom cost? Start exploring our data and see for yourself.

How it works

Data Collection

Every day we pull data from New York City Craigslist apartment listings. This is where we gather prices and other data points like address, neighborhood, etc.

Processing

If you've ever been to Craigslist you know it's a bit...messy. Once we get the raw data we process it by removing outliers, adding additional data like walk scores based on the address, and more.

Visualization

With our processing complete with have some nice and tidy data to share with you. We parse and visualize our data to provide useful insights like price per borough, average sizes of apartments, etc.
